# EchoMind Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-08_

EchoMind is designed to keep your data private. This policy explains what happens
to your information — the short version is: **it stays on your iPhone.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** EchoMind does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. We do
not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind, and there is no account or
sign-in.

## What stays on your device

Everything you create in EchoMind is stored only on your iPhone:

- Audio recordings (if you enable "Keep audio recordings")
- Transcripts of your recordings
- AI-generated summaries, action items, and notes
- Imported documents
- The long-term memory of facts EchoMind builds from your meetings

This data is stored in the app's private, sandboxed storage and protected by iOS
file protection. It is never uploaded to us or to any third party.

## How AI works

Transcription and all AI features (summaries, grouping, memory, and answers) run
**entirely on your device** using Apple's on-device speech recognition and Apple
Intelligence (Foundation Models). Your recordings and text are not sent to any
server — ours or Apple's — for these features.

## Network use

EchoMind makes **no network connections** in normal use. Your content never leaves
your device.

## Permissions

- **Microphone** — used only while you are recording a session, to capture audio
  for transcription.
- **Speech Recognition** — used to transcribe your recordings on-device.

You can revoke these at any time in the iOS Settings app.

## Your control

You can export all of your data, or permanently delete all of it, at any time from
**Settings ▸ Delete All Data / Export All Data** inside EchoMind. Deleting the app
also removes all associated data from your device.

## Children

EchoMind does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update it here and revise the date above.
