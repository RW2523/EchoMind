EchoMind
Private meeting memory for your iPhone

Every meeting, remembered — privately.

Record on your iPhone and EchoMind transcribes live. The moment you stop, it writes a clean report — summary, decisions, action items — names the session from what was discussed, and files it with similar meetings. Then just ask, by text or voice. All of it runs on-device. Nothing ever leaves your iPhone.

100% on-device · no account · no tracking · zero network calls in normal use — enforced by an automated test.

What it does

  • Live transcription — keeps recording while the screen is locked.
  • Auto reports — summary, key decisions, and check-off action items.
  • Smart grouping — similar meetings organize themselves.
  • Total recall — a memory of people, projects, and decisions across every meeting.
  • Ask anything — grounded answers with sources, by text or voice.
  • Voice mode — a hands-free conversation you can interrupt naturally.
  • Reminders & app lock — send action items to Reminders; lock the app with Face ID.

Status

EchoMind is in development. Beta testing runs through TestFlight — if you have an invite link, open it on your iPhone to join.
