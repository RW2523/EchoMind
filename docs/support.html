Common questions

“Transcription isn’t available for my language”

Open Settings ▸ Transcription Language in EchoMind, select your language, and wait for the on-device speech model to download (it needs Wi-Fi once).

No AI summary, or “Requires Apple Intelligence”

Summaries, grouping, memory, and answers use Apple Intelligence. Turn it on in iOS Settings ▸ Apple Intelligence & Siri. On devices that don’t support it, recording, transcription, playback, and export still work — and you can generate the report later once AI is available.

Recording stopped during a phone call

EchoMind pauses for interruptions and resumes automatically. Your transcript up to that point is saved. Recording also continues while the screen is locked.

Microphone access is off

Go to iOS Settings ▸ EchoMind and enable Microphone and Speech Recognition.

The voice reply sounds robotic

EchoMind uses the best voice installed on your iPhone. For a much more natural one, install an enhanced or premium voice in iOS Settings ▸ Accessibility ▸ Spoken Content ▸ Voices, then reopen EchoMind.

How do I get my data out — or delete it?

Settings ▸ Export All Data gives you a Markdown transcript per session plus your audio recordings. Settings ▸ Delete All Data removes everything, including memory and audio. Deleting the app also removes all its data.
