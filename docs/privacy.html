EchoMind
Private meeting memory for your iPhone

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

EchoMind is designed to keep your data private. The short version: it stays on your iPhone.

What we collect

Nothing. EchoMind does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind, and there is no account or sign-in.

What stays on your device

Everything you create in EchoMind is stored only on your iPhone:

  • Audio recordings (if you enable “Keep audio recordings”)
  • Transcripts of your recordings
  • AI-generated summaries, action items, and notes
  • Imported documents
  • The long-term memory of facts EchoMind builds from your meetings

This data is stored in the app’s private, sandboxed storage and protected by iOS file protection. It is never uploaded to us or to any third party.

How AI works

Transcription and all AI features (summaries, grouping, memory, and answers) run entirely on your device using Apple’s on-device speech recognition and Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models). Your recordings and text are not sent to any server — ours or Apple’s — for these features.

Network use

EchoMind makes no network connections in normal use. Your content never leaves your device.

Permissions

  • Microphone — used only while you are recording a session, to capture audio for transcription.
  • Speech Recognition — used to transcribe your recordings on-device.
  • Face ID / Touch ID (optional) — if you turn on the app lock, iOS authenticates you when the app opens. Biometric data is handled entirely by iOS; EchoMind never sees or stores it.
  • Reminders (optional) — only when you tap “Add to Reminders” on a report, to create reminders from your action items. EchoMind writes the reminders you asked for and reads nothing back. Note: reminders you create live in Apple’s Reminders app and follow its settings — if your Reminders sync with iCloud, those items will too. EchoMind itself still sends nothing anywhere.

You can revoke these at any time in the iOS Settings app.

Your control

You can export all of your data, or permanently delete all of it, at any time from Settings ▸ Delete All Data / Export All Data inside EchoMind. Deleting the app also removes all associated data from your device.

Children

EchoMind does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update it here and revise the date above.
